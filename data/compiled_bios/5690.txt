I received Ph.D. degree from Institute of Computing Technology , Chinese Academy of Sciences in 2008, and M.E. degree and B.E. degree from Shandong University, China in 2005 and 2002, respectively. My Ph.D. thesis was titled Computational Conformal Geometry Based Shape Analysis. I have published 90 papers in peer-reviewed journals and conferences and a book by Springer, won two Best paper awards in CAD/CAM 2009 and ICCM 2017, and obtained 4 U.S. patents on the techniques including virtual colonoscopy.
