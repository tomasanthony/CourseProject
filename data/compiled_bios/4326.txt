Andrea Richa ProfessorAndrea W. Richa joined Arizona State University (ASU) in 1998. Prof. Richas work on network algorithms has been widely cited, and includes work on distributed load balancing, packet routing, wireless network modeling and topology control, wireless jamming, data mule networks, underwater optical networking, and distributed hash tables (DHTs). Dr. Richa was the recipient of an NSF CAREER Award in 1999, is currently an Associate Editor of IEEE Transactions on Mobile Computing, and has served as keynote speaker and program\general chair of several prestigious conferences. Prof. Richa has also delivered several invited talks both nationally and internationally.
