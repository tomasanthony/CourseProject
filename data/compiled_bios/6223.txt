Dr. Shawn Bohner brings 23 years of industry and consulting experience to the classroom. An expert in software engineering, he has been the director of the National Science Foundations Center for High Performance Reconfigurable Computing and a research fellow for several organizations. He also has held such positions as vice president, research program director, lead scientist, and senior software engineer for a number of companies. Dr. Bohner directs the software engineering program and also coaches programming teams in Association for Computing Machinery contests.
