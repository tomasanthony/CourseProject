 Kenneth R Brown Associate Professor in the Department of Electrical and Computer Engineering Prof. Brown's research interest is the control of quantum systems for both understanding the natural world and developing new technologies. His current research areas are the development of robust quantum computers and the study of molecular properties at cold and ultracold temperatures.
