Dr. Andrew H. Fagg is an associate professor in the School of Computer Science at the University of Oklahoma. He holds a BS in Applied Mathematics/Computer Science from Carnegie-Mellon University, and a MS and a PhD in Computer Science from the University of Southern California. His research focuses on the computational issues surrounding the symbiotic relationships between humans and machines.
