Abdeslam Boularias is an Assistant Professor in the Computer Science Department at Rutgers University.He received the engineering degree in computer science from the cole Nationale Suprieure dInformatique (ESI) in Algeria in 2004, the Masters degree in computer science from University of Paris-Sud in France in 2005, and the Ph.D. degree from Laval University in Canada in 2010. From August 2010 to April 2013, he was a Research Scientist with the Empirical Inference Department at the Max Planck Institute for Intelligent Systems in Germany. From May 2013 to July 2015, he was a Postdoctoral Fellow and a Project Scientist at Carnegie Mellon University. His main research interests include planning under uncertainty, reinforcement learning, and robotics.
