Fisher College Background My business career began at Northeastern University in the Arts and Sciences Department and expanded while working in the IT division of Houghton Mifflin Harcourt Company. With more than 20 years of experience in information technology, project management, business administration, customer service and theological studies, it has been my responsibility for more than a decade to assist 25 faculty members across multiple departments, in their day-to-day operations. I've had the opportunity to utilize my years of experience, knowledge base, and skills sets to provide efficient, effective, and quality assistance to the faculty, staff, students, administration, and community here at Davidson.
