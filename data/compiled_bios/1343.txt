Yung-Hsiang Lu Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering Professor of Computer Science (courtesy)Joined department: 2004 EducationBSEE, Taiwan University, Electrical Engineering (1992) MSEE, Stanford University, Electrical Engineering (1997) PhD, Stanford University, Electrical Engineering (2002) Dr. Lu's research is developing energy-efficient computing systems using system-level power management. The applications include distributed sensor networks, autonomous robots, wireless communication, and real-time systems. He received the Career Award from National Science Foundation in 2004 for developing advanced energy management in operating systems.
