 David Tipper is a professor at the School of Information Sciences at the University of Pittsburgh where he is also the director of SIS graduate Telecommunications & Networking Program. Tipper has a secondary appointment at Pitts Department of Electrical Engineering. He is a graduate of the University of Arizona (PhD Electrical Engineering, MS Systems Engineering) and Virginia Tech (BS in Electrical Engineering). At Pitt, Tipper teaches courses on communication systems, wireless networks, network performance modeling and analysis, network design, and infrastructure protection. Tippers research has been supported by grants from various government and corporate sources such as NSF, DARPA, NIST, IBM, ARO, and AT&T.
