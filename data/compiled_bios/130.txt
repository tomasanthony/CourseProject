 Daniel A. Spielman I am a Sterling Professor of Computer Science and a Professor Statistics and Data Science and of Mathematics at Yale University . This year I am the acting chair of The Department of Statistics and Data Science . Look here for more information about the major in Statistics and Data Science , or contact the DUS, Sekhar Tatikonda . I am co-Director of the Yale Institute for Network Science (YINS) . I am a Simons Investigator and a MacArthur Fellow . I am a member of the ACM , AMS , IEEE , SIAM , the National Academy of Sciences , and the CT Academy of Science and Engineering .
