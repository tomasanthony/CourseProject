["Gordon Marshall Professor Emeritus of Computer Science, Electrical Engineering-Systems, and Civil and Environmental EngineeringEducation BiographyRequicha is the founding director of the USC Viterbi Laboratory for Molecular Robotics and is known for his pioneering work in nanotechnology. Prior to his nanotechnology research, he helped develop the solid modeling technology widely used in industry, where it has replaced traditional drafting and enabled large improvements in productivity and cost.  He received the Engenheiro Electrotcnico degree from the Instituto Superior Tcnico, Lisbon, Portugal and his Ph.D. in electrical engineering from the University of Rochester.
