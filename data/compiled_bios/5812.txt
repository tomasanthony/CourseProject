Enrollment and Graduation Data Marc Mitchell Biography Assistant Professor of Electrical and Computer Engineering PhD, Cornell University Koch Center Room 263 812-488-2033 mm73@evansville.edu Professor Mitchell is an assistant professor of electrical and computer engineering at the University of Evansville. Mitchell is actively involved in research to develop diagnostics for pulsed power driven plasmas. This research stems from his background in novel x-ray sources from plasma radiation sources. Professor Mitchell is also interested in microcontroller applications and leads many projects in robotics, automation and other microcontroller based systems.
