Dr. Aaron Wilkin specializes in distributed systems and complex event processing systems. His research activities include transactions on Internet technology, and providing strong guarantees on messages and events in an event-based distributed system. This is his second stint in the department, having served as a guest instructor in the spring of 2013. Dr. Wilkin has advanced knowledge of C, Java, and Python computing languages and also helped NCR develop entire test frameworks, simulators, and test generators.
