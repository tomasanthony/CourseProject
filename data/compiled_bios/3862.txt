Thomas Rothvoss joined UW CSE in January 2015 as a joint faculty appointment with the UW Department of Mathematics. He arrived at UW Math in 2014 following a postdoc at MIT. Thomas is a 2015 Sloan Research Fellow and earned Best Paper awards at STOC 2014, SODA 2014, and STOC 2010. His research interests include discrete optimization, linear/integer programming and theoretical computer science. Thomas received his Ph.D. from the cole Polytechnique Fdrale de Lausanne following his studies at the Universitt Paderborn and the Technische Universitt Dortmund. To learn more, visit his website .
