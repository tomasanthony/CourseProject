John Silvester is Professor of Electrical Engineering at the University of Southern California and has served as Vice-Provost for Scholarly Technology and Vice-Provost for Academic Computing. He was also the Chairman of the Board of the Corporation for Educational Network Initiatives in California (CENIC). Research SummaryRecent work has focussed on traffic analysis and capacity planning for multi-media traffic.  PI on a NSF Cyber-Infrastructure International Research Network Connections project.  Actively involved in the development of the next generation internet in California, the US, and Internationally.
