Miscellany B.S., Case Institute of Technology, 1966 M.S., Ph.D., Stanford University, 1967, 1972 Joined Yale Faculty 1971 Major research interests include numerical linear and nonlinear algebra, direct and iterative methods for solving sparse linear systems, and parallel computing.
