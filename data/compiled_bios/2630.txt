 Junhui Hou's Homepage Biography People Professional Service and Awards Research Teaching Biography Junhui HOU , Ph.D. Assistant Professor Department of Computer Science City University of Hong Kong (CityU) Address : Y6427, 6/F, Yeung Kin Man Academic Building, #83 Tat Chee Avenue, Kowloon, Hong Kong Email : jh 'dot' hou 'at' cityu 'dot' edu 'dot' hk Tel : +852 3442 8498; Fax : +852 3442 0503 Junhui Hou was born in Xin'xiang , Henan Province, China, in 1988. He received the B.Eng. degree in information engineering (Talented Students Program) from the South China University of Technology (SCUT) , Guangzhou, China, in 2009, the M.Eng. degree in signal and information processing from Northwestern Polytechnical University (NPU) , Xian, China, in 2012, and the Ph.D. degree from the School of Electrical and Electronic Engineering, Nanyang Technological University (NTU), Singapore, in 2016, respectively. He joined the Department of Computer Science, CityU as an Assistant Professor in Jan. 2017. Hisresearch interests fall into the general areas of visual signal processing, such as adaptive image/video representations, learning based motion capture data and depth video analysis, 3-D geometrical data representations, graph signal processing, discriminative modeling for clustering/classification, and hyperspectral image analysis. He was the recipient of the prestigious award of Chinese Government for Outstanding Self-financed Students Abroad in 2015 from China Scholarship Council and Early Career Award in 2018 from Hong Kong Research Grants Council (RGC) .
