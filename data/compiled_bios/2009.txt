 Yi-King Choi Department of Computer Science Pokfulam Road The University of Hong Kong Hong Kong Email ykchoi@cs.hku.hk Offine phone +852 2857 8448 Fax +852 2559 8447 Bio Yi-King Choi received her BSc (with first class honors) in 1996 from the University of Hong Kong. She continued her graduate studies there under the direction of Professor Wenping Wang, and earned her MPhil and PhD degrees in 2000 and 2008, respectively. She was awarded the Li Ka Shing Prize for Best PhD Theses by HKU in 2009. She is currently a research assistant professor of computer science at the University of Hong Kong.
