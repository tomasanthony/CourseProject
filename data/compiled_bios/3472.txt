Dr. Daniel Moores areas of expertise include engineering design, electronics, engineering educational methods, engineering ethics and international design projects. He is involved in undergraduate capstone design projects and international team-based projects, and is co-director of dual masters degree program with the University of Applied Sciences in Ulm, Germany. Professionally, he is associate editor of the Advances in Engineering Education online, peer-reviewed journal, has been a program chair for the Frontiers in Education Conference the division chair for the American Society of Engineering Educations Educational Research Materials meetings, and is an ABET program evaluator at national and international locations. Dr. Moore co-advises the Alpha Phi Omega service organization.
