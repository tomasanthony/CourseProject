AmlieMarian is an Associate Professor in theComputer Science DepartmentatRutgers University. Her research interests are in Personal Information Management, Ranked Query Processing, Semi-structured data and Web data Management.Amliereceived her Ph.D. in Computer Science fromColumbia Universityin 2005. From March 1999 to August 2000,Amliewas a member of the VERSO project atINRIA-Rocquencourt. She received B.S. and M.S. degrees fromUniversity Paris Dauphine, France in 1998 and 1999, respectively.She is the recipient of a Microsoft Live Labs Award (2006), three Google Research Awards (2008, 2010, and 2012) and an NSF CAREER award (2009).
