Dr. Sridhar Radhakrishnan is a professor and the Director of the School of Computer Science at the University of Oklahoma, where he joined in 1990. He received his PhD in Computer Science from Louisiana State University in 1990.Dr. Radhakrishnans research interests are in algorithm development and Computer Networks. His work has resulted in many protocol development for broadband networks, wireless and mobile networks, and RFID Systems. He is currently working on the development of protocols at the intersection of of Internet-of-things (IOT) and Software Defined Networks. He also actively involved in the development of algorithms for high performance computing, especially on desktop parallel computing systems. He has published over 100 research articles in journals, conference proceedings, and book chapters.
