Research Overview I'm interested in all aspects of network and system security. To solve practical security problems, I use networking and system techniques, as well as applied cryptography, machine learning, artificial intelligence, probability/statistics, information theory, etc. My current specific research interests include: (see details in relevant publications by topic ) Internet malware/botnet/APT detection, defense, and analysis SPS: Software-defined programmable security (SDN, NFV, Cloud, Edge, ...)
