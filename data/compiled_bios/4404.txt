Ran Canetti is a professor of Computer Science at Boston University and the director of the center for Reliable Information System and Cyber Security. He is also a Fellow of the International Association for Cryptologic Research and an associate editor of the Journal of Cryptology and Information and Computation. Canetti graduated from the Weizmann Institute of Science, was a researcher at IBM Watson Research Center, a research scientist at MIT and a professor at Tel Aviv University. Canettis research interests span multiple aspects of cryptography and information security, with emphasis on the design, analysis and use of cryptographic protocols.
