About me I received the Diploma and Ph.D. degrees in 2001 and 2006 from the School of Electrical and Computer Engineering in NTUA . I worked as a postdoc in UCLA between 2006 and 2008, and then joined Ecole Centrale Paris as Assistant Professor in the Department of Applied Mathematics, and the GALEN research team of INRIA-Saclay . Starting from 2016 I am with UCL's department of Computer Science and Facebook's Artificial Intelligence Research (FAIR) team. My current research interests are computer vision (detection, segmentation, shape modelling) and machine learning (deep learning, structured prediction). 2007 All Rights Reserved.
