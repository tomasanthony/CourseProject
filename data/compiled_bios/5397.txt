 Ewan Klein Navigation Home Publications Projects Students I am Professor Emeritus of Language Technology in the University of Edinburghs School of Informatics . From September 2017, I am also a Turing Fellow at the Alan Turing Institute . Research My research career spans a range of topics, including theoretical linguistics; computational phonology, syntax and semantics; spoken dialogue with mobile robots; text mining in domains such as medical biology and digital history; the use, interpretation and social embedding of different forms of data in the context of Living Labs and the Internet of Things. Current Initiatives Edinburgh Living Lab I am a Co-Founder and Co-Director of Edinburgh Living Lab , an organisation that focuses on data and design thinking to stimulate innovation, developed in collaboration with the City of Edinburgh Council and a range of public, third and private sector partners. University IoT Service I am the Programme Sponsor for the Universitys Internet of Things Initiative . We are building the facilities and supporting services that will enable research and teaching at the University to safely and responsibly explore the rapidly expanding world of connected devices. Prewired I am a Founder and the Chair of the Board of Trustees of Prewired , a Scottish Charity that provides an unstructured but supportive environment for under 19s to explore and learn about computer programming and related topics.
