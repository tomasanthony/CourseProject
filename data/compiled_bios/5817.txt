I am an Associate Professor in the Computer Science Department of the Texas Tech University (TTU) in Lubbock, Texas. I am the founding Director of the Data-Intensive Scalable Computing Laboratory (DISCL). I am also the Associate Director of the Cloud and Autonomic Computing site at TTU (CAC@TTU). My research focuses on data-intensive computing, high-performance computing, parallel and distributed computing, cloud computing, computer architectures, and systems software support for high-performance scientific computing/high-end enterprise computing.
