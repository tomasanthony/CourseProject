Enrollment and Graduation Data Christina Howe Biography After graduating from the University of Evansville in 2003 in computer engineering, Professor Howe attended graduate school at Vanderbilt University. In 2009 she returned to UE to teach and is now an associate professor of electrical engineering. She enjoys mentoring students in and out of the classroom and serves as the SWE co-advisor. She also serves as the Affiliate Director representing UE for the Indiana Space Grant Consortium. She has published papers related to semiconductor electronics and engineering education.
