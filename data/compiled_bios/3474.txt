Dr. Niusha Rostamkolais areas of specialization cover power engineering and controlengineering, including power system transmission, generation planning, stability and protection, alternative energy and control systems. He has industry experience working for General Electric and has been a consultant for American Electric Power, Duke Energy, Midcontinent Independent System Operators, Indianapolis Power & Light, Cummins and Caterpillar. Dr. Rostamkolai is a past-chair of the Institute of Electrical and Electronic Engineerscentral Indiana section.
