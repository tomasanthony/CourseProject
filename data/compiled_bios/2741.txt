 Maryam Kamali University of Liverpool Home Research Publications Contact About me I am a research associate at the University of Liverpool working with Prof. Michael Fisher . Currently I am involved in the Verifiable Autonomy project. I am also a member of the Logic and Computation research group . I received a Ph.D. in Computer Science from Abo Akademi University , Finland in September 2013. My research focus was on formal specification and verification of networked systems, using theorem prover and model checker.
