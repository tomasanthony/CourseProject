Hank Streeter received his BS and MS degrees in applied mathematics, with an emphasis on continuum mechanics, from Brown University. He has over twenty years experience in scientific and technical software development. He spent four years in climate research at Brown, nine years in the oil and gas industry in Houston, and nine years in telecoms-related software development before coming to CTI in 2001. In addition, Hank taught object-oriented modeling at Rice University part-time for five years.
