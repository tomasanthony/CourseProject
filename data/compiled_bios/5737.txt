Her research investigated computational models of argumentation. She has taught a variety of undergraduate classes in the Computer Science Department at UO, including upper and lower division and introductory computer science courses, as well as a teaching effectiveness seminar for graduate students. Prior to coming to Oregon, Kathleen worked in the computer industry as a programmer/analyst at Shared Medical Systems in Malvern, PA.
