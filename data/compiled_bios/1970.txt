Wong-Ma Associate Teaching Professor, SOE 3062 Bren Hall Computer Science Dept, UC Irvine Fall 2018 Office Hours: Tues 9:30-11am, Thurs 12-1pm In Fall 2018, I joined the UC Irvine Computer Science Department as a Associate Teaching Professor, SOE. Prior to joing UCIrvine, I was a teaching faculty, an undergraduate academic advisor, and the 5-year BS/MS program advisor in the Computer Science Department at Stony Brook University. I mainly taught sophomore and junior level Computer Science systems and architecture courses. I received the CS Department Award for Undergraduate Education in 2012 and CS Department Award for Major Contributions to Undergraduate Education in 2016. I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science from UCLA. My research interests include wireless and distributed embedded systems, hardware intellectual property protection, and statistical optimization.
