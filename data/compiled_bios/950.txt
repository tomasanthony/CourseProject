Brief Biography Allan Jepson received his B.Sc. in 1976 from the University of British Columbia and his Ph.D. in Applied Mathematics in 1980 from the California Institute of Technology. He then moved to a postdoctoral position in the Mathematics Department at Stanford University. In 1982 he joined the faculty at the Department of Computer Science at the University of Toronto, where he has been a full professor since 1991. His current research interests span a range of topics in computer vision, including image grouping, image segmentation, and motion estimation.
