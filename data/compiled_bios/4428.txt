Alley Stoughton is a Research Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Boston University, as well as a Visiting Fellow at BUs Hariri Institute for Computing and Computational Science & Engineering. She has a background in programming language semantics, having received her doctorate in computer science from the University of Edinburgh in 1987. Her current research focus is on the application of formal methods to security. Current projects include: https://alleystoughton.us/
