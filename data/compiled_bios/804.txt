Dr. Montague is currently an Associate Professor in the college of computing at DePaul University and adjunct professor at Northwestern Universitys Feinberg School of Medicine. She received MS and PhD degrees in Industrial and Systems Engineering from Virginia Tech, specializing in human factors and ergonomics engineering and human computer interaction.  Dr. Montague has received numerous awards for her research including the Francis Research Fellowship for research that emphasizes longer, safer and healthier lives and a Kl2 award from that National Institutes of Health (NIH) to explore trust in health care systems. She has over 100 peer reviewed publications on topics at the intersection of human technology interaction and healthcare and is currently the director of the Wellness and Health Enhancement Engineering Laboratory (WHEEL).
