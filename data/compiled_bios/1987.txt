National Chiao-Tung University, Taiwan Bio Chun-Hsi Huang joined the Computer Science faculty of the American University in September 2018. Prior to coming to AU, he was on the Computer Science and Engineering faculty of the University of Connecticut, Storrs. His research interests lie in the areas of Extreme-Scale Computing and Data Analytics, Computational Biology and Life Science Informatics, as well as Combinatorial Algorithms and Experimental Algorithmics. :
