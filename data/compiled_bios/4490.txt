 Xu Chu Office: Klaus 3322 Address: School of Computer Science Georgia Institute of Technology 266 Ferst Drive, Atlanta, GA 30332-0765 Email: xu.chu@cc.gatech.edu I am an assistant professor in the School of Computer Science at the Georgia Institute of Technology. I completed my PhD program in the Data Systems Lab at the University of Waterloo in 2017, and I obtained my bachelor's degree in Computer Science from Nanjing University in 2011. I lead the Chu Data Lab , where we work on practical and challenging problems in data management and machine learning. I am looking for motivated graduate students. If you are interested in doing research at the intersection of data management and machine learning, feel free to send me an email.
