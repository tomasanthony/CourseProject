Biography Derick joined the Department of Computer Science, HKUST as Professor on January 2, 1995 and have been our faculty for over 10 years until his retirement on June 30, 2006. During the period September 2001 to June 2002, Derick was appointed as Acting Head of the Department. He was conferred the title Chair Professor in January 2006, and has been our Professor Emeritus since his retirement.
