Lawrence M. Wolk About me Mr. Lawrence Wolk is an Instructor of Computer and Information Science at Fordham University. He has previously served in the capacity of a Computer Graphics and Imaging Systems consultant and in this role he has developed numerous computer simulations in connection with Electromagnetic Sensor Design, Biomedical Imaging, and Interactive Platform Data Display. Additionally, he specializes in the evaluation of educational credentials for foreign students and prepares individual educational equivalency assessments for outside firms. He previously worked as a Senior Engineer for Riverside Research Institute, Raytheon Systems and Grumman Aerospace Systems. Mr. Wolk completed his M.S.E.E. degree at Polytechnic Institute of New York and an undergraduate degree in Mathematics at Fordham University. - Contact Info Office: JMH 308B, Fordham University, 441 E.
