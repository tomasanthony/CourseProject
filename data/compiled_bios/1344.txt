Cristina Nita-Rotaru Adjunct Professor of Computer ScienceJoined department: 2003 EducationBS, Politehnica University of Bucharest, Computer Science (1995) MS, Politehnica University of Bucharest, Computer Science (1996) MSE, The Johns Hopkins University, Computer Science (2000) PhD, The Johns Hopkins University, Computer Science (2003) Cristina Nita-Rotaru joined Purdue in 2003, where she conducts her research within the Dependable and Secure Distributed Systems Laboratory (DS^2). Cristina Nita-Rotaru's research interests lie in designing distributed systems and network protocols and applications that are dependable and secure, while maintaining acceptable levels of performance.
