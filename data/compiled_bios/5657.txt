Brandon Dixon Associate Professor Computer Science Department University of Alabama 205-348-0598 205-348-0219 (FAX) dixon@cs.ua.edu Brandon Dixon has been with the Department since the Fall of 1994. His research interests include algorithms and software engineering (component engineering). He graduated from the Vanderbilt University with a B.S. in Computer Science in 1989, received his M.S. and Ph.D. in Computer Science from Princeton University in 1993.
