Dr. Wu received his BS in Mechanical Engineering from the Nanjing University of Aeronautics &amp; Astronautics in 1998 and his Ph.D. in Electrical and Computer Engineering from the University of Nevada, Las Vegas in 2007. At CSULB, he teaches introductory undergraduate courses such as logic design and IC design in the Computer Engineering program. Dr. Wu's research interests include network on chip/system on chip design, IC design, interconnection network, and reliability modeling. He is an IEEE member.
