Dr. Bo Fu joined the Department of Computer Engineering and Computer Science at California State University, Long Beach in Fall 2015. Dr. Fu holds a Ph.D in Computer Science(2011) from Trinity College Dublin. Prior to joining CSULB, she worked for the Stanford National Center for Biomedical Ontology and the University of Victoria. Dr Fu's research focuses on knowledge-enabled and data centric computing and its application in health informatics. She is interested in cross-disciplinary research solving issues in predictive and participtory medicine using technologies and techniques from the Semantic Web, data engineering, data analytics, as well as human-computer interaction.
