 Degrees Ph.D. in Computer Science (Human-Computer Interaction), University of Waterloo, Canada M.F.A. in Film, York University, Canada M.A. in Communication Studies, Wilfrid Laurier University, Canada Favorite Spot on Campus MPC studio Bio Krzysztof is an Assistant Professor in the Film and Media Arts Division of the School of Communication at American University in Washington, DC.
