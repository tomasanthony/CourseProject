Wes Weimer Associate Professor, Computer Science (by Courtesy) Bio UC Berkeley, 2005 Westley Weimer was a faculty member at the Department of Computer Science from 2006 - 2017 and now holds a courtesy appointment. Westley Weimer received his Ph.D. from UC Berkeley in 2005 where he worked on programming languages and reliability with his advisor George Necula. He has been on the faculty of the University of Virignia department of Computer Science in 2006. Research Interests Automated program repair Static and dynamic analyses for software quality and reliability Contact Information Wes Weimer Computer Science wrw6y@virginia.edu
