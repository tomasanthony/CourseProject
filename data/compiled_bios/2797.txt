Baranoski is a Professor at the School	of Computer Science, University of Waterloo, Canada.	His current research interests include the predictive simulation of	light interactions with organic materials and the	biophysically-based rendering of natural phenomena. The results of	his	research have been made available to the scientific	community through the publication of articles in journals and	conference proceedings in computer graphics and related fields	such as applied optics and remote sensing.
