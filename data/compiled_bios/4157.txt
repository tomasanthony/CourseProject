Aidong Zhang Professor, Computer Science and Biomedical Engineering Bio PhD, Purdue 1994 Aidong Zhang is a William Wulf Faculty Fellow and Professor of Computer Science and Biomedical Engineering in the School of Engineering and Applied Sciences at University of Virginia. She is also affiliated with the Data Science Institute at University of Virginia. Her research interests focus on data mining/data science, machine learning, bioinformatics and health informatics.
