Enrollment and Graduation Data Tony Richardson Biography Professor Richardson is an associate professor of electrical engineering at the University of Evansville. He has been a member of the faculty at the University of Evansville since 2000. Education Ph.D. - Duke University 1990 M.S.E.E. - Syracuse University 1984 B.S.E.E. - University of Kentucky 1981 Experience Edison Engineer, General Electric, Syracuse, NY Research Assistant Professor, Duke University Oceanographer, Scripps Institute of Oceanography, San Diego, CA Specialty Areas Digital Signal Processing Electronic Communication Professional Memberships IEEE ASEE Peer Reviewed Journal Publications EXERCISES IN CONTINUOUS-TIME CONVOLUTION, Anthony Richardson, ASEE National Conference, Columbus, OH, June 2017 TEACHING LINEAR SYSTEMS USING MASTERY INSTRUCTION, Anthony Richardson, ASEE National Conference, Louisville, KY, June 2010 USING SIMULATION AS AN AID IN UNDERSTANDING ENGINEERING STATISTICS, Anthony Richardson, ASEE National Conference, Louisville, KY, June 2010 CLASSIFICATION AND ASSESSMENT OF PROJECTS IN COMPUTER ENGINEERING, Dick Blandford, Christina Howe, Anthony Richardson, David Mitchell, ASEE National Conference, Louisville, KY, June 2010 A REAL TIME COURSE FOR COMPUTER ENGINEERS, Anthony Richardson, Dick Blandford, ASEE National Conference, Salt Lake City, UT, June 2004 AN ONLINE UNIX SYSTEM PROGRAMMING COURSE FOR COMPUTER ENGINEERING STUDENTS, Anthony Richardson, ASEE National Conference, Salt Lake City, UT, June 2004 Return to the list of faculty .
