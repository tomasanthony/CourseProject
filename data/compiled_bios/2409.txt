Department of Computer Science Search form Search Main menu Main Menu About CS Academics Research Outreach People Events Industry News Open Positions Support CS Internal You are here Home George Strawn Emeritus People Professor Emeritus George Strawn (EAB chair) is a former Department Chair of Computer Science, Director of the Computation Center at ISU, Professor. He then spent a number of years in various positions at NSF, that last of which was on detail to OSTP as co-chair of the Networking and IT Research and Development (NITRD) subcommittee of the National Science and Technology Council and also as director of the National Coordination Office, which staffs the NITRD subcommittee. After retiring from NSF, he recently became board director for the National Academies' Board on Research Data and Information.
