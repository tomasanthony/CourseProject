 Welcome Vitae Publications Photos Contactinfo Zoran Budimli My name is Zoran Budimli, I am a Senior Research Scientist in the Department of Computer Science at Rice University in Houston, TX. My research interests include compilers for high productivity languages, object-oriented languages and compilation, high performance and parallel computing, software transactional memory and more. Anything that will help the programmers to write code for the increasingly powerful (but also increasingly complex) computers of today and tomorrow. I am a member of the Habanero Multicore Software Project at Rice University, whose goal is to develop new programming technologies that improve productivity and performance of software on multicore machines.
