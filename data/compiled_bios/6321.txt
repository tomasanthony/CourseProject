Prof. Richard Bonert Dipl.Ing.(Karlsruhe), Ph.D.(Karlsruhe) bonert@ecf.utoronto.ca 10 Kings College Road, Room 1020A, Toronto, Ontario, Canada. Research Interests Industrial systems in Power-Electronics and Motion Control with and including computer and microelectronic circuits based automation. Examples: 15 Farad 230V 400kWsec energy storage system, 80 kHz resonance link converter system for transportation, 1 MhZ 1kW DC/AC converter, Realtime controller systems based on digital signal processors (DSP) and programmable logic devices (PLD) with up to 4 Gflops of computing power, sophisticated AC-machine controls.
