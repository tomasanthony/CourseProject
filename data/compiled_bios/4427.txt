Aaron has been a full-time Lecturer in the Department of Computer Science at Boston University since 2004, where he has taught introductory classes for both computer science majors and non-majors. Aarons teaching emphasis has been in teaching computer programming and web application development. In addition, Aaron developed and taught a class in personal life-cycle economics that ran from 2009-2013, and currently teaches a class in mathematical modeling for personal finance in the Department of Mathematics.
