["Stephen and Etta Varra Professor and Professor of Computer Science and Electrical Engineering-SystemsEducationDoctoral Degree, Computer Science, University of California - Los AngelesMaster's Degree, Computer Science, University of California - Los AngelesBachelor's Degree, Computer Science, University of California - Los Angeles BiographyLeana Golubchik is a Professor of Computer Science, with a joint appointment in Electrical Engineering, at USC. Prior to that she was an Assistant Professor and then an Associate Professor at the University of Maryland at College Park and an Assistant Professor at Columbia University. Leana received her Ph.D. from UCLA. Her research interests are broadly in the design and evaluation of large scale distributed systems, with emphasis on analysis techniques needed for rapid evaluation of design ideas that lead to insight about systems' performance and reliability. She is a past Chair, Vice Chair, and Board of Directors member of ACM SIGMETRICS (special interest group on performance evaluation), and a past editor of the ACM Performance Evaluation Review. She has served as a program co-chair and general chair of ACM SIGMETRICS, program chair of Performance, program co-chair of MIS, a co-chair of HotMetrics, and is also on the Editorial Board of the Performance Evaluation journal and the ACM Transactions on Modeling and Performance Evaluation of Computing Systems. Leana received several awards, including the IBM Faculty Award, the NSF CAREER award, the Okawa Foundation award, and the IBM & NSF Doctoral Fellowships. She is a member of the IFIP WG 7.3 (elected in 2000) and Tau Beta Pi.
