 Vikash Singh Assistant Professor of the Practice of Computer Science Electrical Engineering and Computer Science Biography Dr. Vikash Singhs research interests include Human-Computer Interaction, collaboration tools and computer science pedagogy. His recent research has focused on team-based and video-centered active learning. He has received National Science Foundation I-corps and STTR grants to study, develop and commercialize tools for detailed and accurate discussion of video material targeting the flipped classroom model for STEM higher education.
