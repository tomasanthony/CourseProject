I am part of the Theory group. Research Interests I am interested in all aspects of Theoretical Computer Science. I have worked on Complexity Theory, Combinatorics and Graph Algorithms. Education Ph.D. in Algorithms, Combinatorics & Optimization from College of Computing , Georgia Tech (2011). I was fortunate to have Dick Lipton and Asaf Shapira as my Ph.D. advisors. M.S. in Mathematics from Georgia Tech (2009). M.E. in Telecommunication Engineering from Indian Institute of Science (2003). B.Tech. in Electronics and Communication Engineering, NIT Calicut (2001).
