I have a broad interest in theoretical computer science, discrete mathematics, probability, and the interplay between them. In particular, I am interested in local-to-global phenomena in combinatorial objects, complex networks, probability theory, and their applications. I am looking for MSc and PhD students in theoretical computer science. If you are interested in working with me, please email me and apply to our graduate program .
