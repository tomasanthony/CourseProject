In addition, I've supervised over 30 independent studies, including three honors theses. My professional and outreach activities include the Charlotte Math Club , AP Calculus (including NCAAPMT ), and the Mathematical Association of America ( MAA ) I also collaborate with Professor Bivens on new editions of the Anton-Bivens-Davis Calculus textbook.
