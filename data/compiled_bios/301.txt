 Toggle navigation Home Vita Research Teaching Theses Eileen T. Kraemer, PhD Graduate coordinator, Ph.D. program in Human-Centered Computing Dr. Eileen Kraemer joined the faculty at Clemson as the C. Tycho Howle Director of the School of Computing in the summer of 2014. In the spring of 2018 she transitioned to the role of grad coordinator for the HCC PhD program. Prior to her time at Clemson, she served as Professor of Computer Science and Associate Dean of the Franklin College of Arts & Sciences at the University of Georgia. Kraemers research focuses on the intersection of humans and technology, with two major thrusts: human aspects of software development (CS education and software engineering) and human aspects of bioinformatics (design of tools and user interfaces for bioinformatics).
