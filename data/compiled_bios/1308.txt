Chunyi Peng Assistant Professor of Computer Science Joined department: Fall 2017 EducationPh.D, University of California, Los Angeles, Computer Science (2013) Chunyi Peng's research interests are in the broad areas of mobile networking, system and security, with a recent focus on 4G/5G mobile network architecture, protocols, and technologies for emerging/demanding applications, mobile network/system security, network data analytics, and mobile sensing and computing systems.
