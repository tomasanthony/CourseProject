Enrollment and Graduation Data Mark Randall Biography Assistant Professor of Electrical Engineering MS, University of Evansville Koch Center Room 274 812-488-2498 mr63@evansville.edu Mr. Randall is an assistant professor of electrical engineering at the University of Evansville. He is the faculty advisor for the student chapters of Institute of Electrical and Electronics Engineering (IEEE) and Eta Kappa Nu (HKN). Mr. Randall is heavily involved in community outreach, professional consulting, and community service. Mr. Randall has been the Middle School Options for Boys camp director for 10 years.
