More news... Bio ( CV ) I am an assistant professor and Dean's faculty fellow ofcomputer science in the School of Computing at Clemson University . I received my Ph.D. degree in Computer Science and Engineering from Arizona State University (ASU) . I was one of the founding members of the Laboratory of Security Engineering for Future Computing ( SEFCOM ) at ASU. My primary research interests are in computer security and privacy. My research has been funded by NSF (SaTC, CNS, IIS, OAC, and DGE ), USDOT , VMware, Amazon, Dell, etc. I received the NSF CAREER Award in 2019. I am the recipient of the Best Paper Awards from ACM SIGCSE 2018 and ACM CODASPY 2014, and the Best Paper Award Honorable Mentions from ACM SACMAT 2016, IEEE ICNP 2015, and ACM SACMAT 2011. My research has won the First Place in ACM SIGCOMM 2018 SRC .
