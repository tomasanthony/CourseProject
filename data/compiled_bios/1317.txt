Roopsha Samanta Assistant Professor of Computer ScienceJoined department: Fall 2016 EducationPh.D., The University of Texas at Austin, Electrical and Computer Engineering (2013) Roopsha Samanta is an Assistant Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Purdue University. Her research mission is to make it easier for people to build provably reliable programs. Her research focuses on developing algorithms and tools for automated program repair and synthesis, and targets diverse application domains such as concurrent and distributed systems, personalized education and machine learning. Roopsha completed her Ph.D. at The University of Texas at Austin in 2013 and was a postdoctoral researcher at the Institute of Science and Technology Austria (IST Austria) from 2014-2016.
