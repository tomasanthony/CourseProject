 Dr. Michael (Eonsuk) Shin Associate Professor Department of Computer Science Texas Tech University Box 43104 Lubbock, TX 79413-3104 Office: 311, Engineering Center Phone: (806) 742-3527 | Fax: (806) 742-3519 Email: michael.shin@ttu.edu Biosketch Dr. Michael Shin is Associate Professor in the Department of Computer Science at Texas Tech University, Lubbock, Texas. He received his B.Sc. in Industrial Engineering from Korea University, Seoul, Korea in 1985 and M.Sc. in Management Science from Korea Advanced Institute of Science and Technology (KAIST), Seoul, Korea in 1988 and Ph.D. in Information Technology with specialization in Software Engineering from George Mason University, Virginia, USA in Spring 2002. From 1988 to 2002, He worked for Electronics and Telecommunications Research Institute (ETRI), Daejeon, Korea as a senior software researcher in the software engineering division.
