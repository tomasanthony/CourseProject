He has strong interest in the application of theoretical disciplines to the solving of engineering problems in terms of the design and analysis of optimal and near optimal algorithms. While at IBM Watson Research Center in his early career, he was engaged in industrial research projects related to compiler code optimization, code compressions, and VLSI circuit testing.
