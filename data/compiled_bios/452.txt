Top Dr. Sun Aixin is an Associate Professor and Assistant Chair (Admissions and Outreach) with School of Computer Science and Engineering ( SCSE ), Nanyang Technological University ( NTU ), Singapore. He received B.A.Sc (1st class honours) and Ph.D. both in Computer Engineering from the same school in 2001 and 2004 respectively. Aixin's research areas include Information Retrivel, Text Mining, Social Computing, and Digital Libraries. Many of his papers appear in leading international conferences and journals in the related areas. Aixin is a member of editorial board for JASIST and Information Retrieval Journal. He has been a PC member of many major conferences such as SIGIR, KDD, and WWW.
