RonCytron's research interests include automatic program optimization and transformation (especially of network software and middleware), voting strategies suitable for the Internet and storage-management systems suitable for object-oriented programs.. Professor Cytron was a Research Staff Member at IBM's Thomas J. Watson Research Center from 1984 to 1993 before he joined the faculty at Washington University in St. Louis..
